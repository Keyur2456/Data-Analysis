About Dataset¶

The dataset includes information on air quality and the AQI (Air Quality Index) at the hourly and daily levels from numerous stations located throughout various Indian cities. Cities covered in this dataset are Ahmedabad, Aizawl, Amaravati, Amritsar, Bengaluru, Bhopal, Brajrajnagar, Chandigarh, Chennai, Coimbatore, Delhi, Ernakulam, Gurugram, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Jorapokhar, Kochi, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mumbai, Patna, Shillong, Talcher, Thiruvananthapuram, Visakhapatnam. The dataset for air quality of India is compared with the dataset for air quality in Madrid.
